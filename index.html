What we do

Perceived value

Luxury hospitality consulting

Beautiful rooms alone do not command premium prices. Believed brands do. We work on what makes a guest decide your nights are worth more: positioning, experience design, pricing coherence. The rate is not the goal. It is the consequence.

Growth

Who’s behind it

Built by an operator, not an outsider.

Mattia Ferro
Founder, Vimana Holidays · CEO, Luxury Camp at Union Lido · Travel advisor, Virtuoso network
Virtuoso profile Essays on hospitality LinkedIn Instagram Luxury Camp at Union Lido Open Air Vacanze

Mattia started with the part everyone skips: selling. A marketer first, he studied how brands are built, then founded a company that imported American Airstreams and sold bungalows on wheels across Italy. He saw open air could speak the language of luxury before anyone else did.

Then the slow part. Years studying the world’s best hotels, from Dubai to Las Vegas to Shanghai. Five more earning the trust of Union Lido’s owners. In 2018 the two worlds merged into Luxury Camp at Union Lido, created in partnership with Union Lido and run by the group’s participated company, Camping Management Srl. Italy’s first five-star open-air resort, later recognised by Forbes.

Running it changed his definition of the job. Hosting six hundred families a year taught him what guests actually pay for. Not marble. Time and attention. So he crossed the desk: as a travel advisor in the Virtuoso network, he now designs journeys for the same kind of guest he hosts.

Vimana is where it all converges: brand and digital marketing for hospitality and lifestyle projects, consulting for operators who want their nights to be worth more, and travel designed by someone who runs the other side. He writes about it at mattiaferro.com.
