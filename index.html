Who’s behind it

Built by an operator, not an outsider.

Mattia Ferro
Founder, Vimana Holidays · CEO, Luxury Camp at Union Lido

Mattia started out as a marketer. After studying branding, he founded a company selling bungalows on wheels, the mobile homes that put him inside the open-air business for good.

Then he joined two worlds that rarely meet: the comfort of high-end hospitality and the freedom of open air. In 2018 that idea became Luxury Camp at Union Lido, created in partnership with Union Lido and run by the group’s participated company, Camping Management Srl. Italy’s first five-star open-air resort, later recognised by Forbes.

Today Vimana is how that path is put to work: brand and digital marketing for hospitality and lifestyle projects, and consulting for the ones that want to charge what their experience is worth. He writes about it at mattiaferro.com.
